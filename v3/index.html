Who We Are?

Innovo Group started as an agricultural equipment and chemical supply company with dynamic and motivated professionals. We have since expanded to serve industrial manufacturers and distributors from Sarawak and Brunei.

Our expertise spans across pharmaceutical, agricultural, food manufacturing, lab grade chemicals, lab instruments supply, and comprehensive logistics services.
