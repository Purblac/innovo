Company Overview

Who We Are?

Founded in 2019, Innovo Synergy started as an agricultural equipment and chemical supply company. Our team consists of dynamic and motivated professionals. We make fast and clear decisions based on our experience and market knowledge.

Our clients are industrial manufacturers and distributors primarily from Sarawak and Brunei. We serve various industries such as pharmaceutical, agricultural for the production of fertilisers, food manufacturing, preparation of lab grade chemicals and supply of lab instruments.
