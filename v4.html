About Us

Who WeAre

Founded in 2019, Innovo Group began as an agricultural equipment and chemical supply company. Today, we are a diversified solutions provider serving industrial manufacturers and distributors across multiple sectors.

Our team consists of experienced, motivated professionals who make fast, informed decisions based on deep market knowledge and technical expertise.
